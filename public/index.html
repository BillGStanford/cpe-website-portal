The Manifesto

Systemic change requires absolute, uncompromising demands.

01 / SEIZE

Expropriate Big Energy

Take the entire energy sector out of private control. Run it as a fully democratized, green public utility managed directly by the workers.

02 / LIBERATE

De-Commodify Housing

Ban corporate landlords and speculative housing. Enforce immediate rent freezes and construct high-quality, state-funded social housing.

03 / EMPOWER

Democratize the Workplace

Dismantle the corporate hierarchy. Put decision-making, profits, and operations directly into the hands of collective union assemblies.
